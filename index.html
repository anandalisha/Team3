About Us

Experience has shown us that frightening and depressing messages often put others off the idea of helping the voiceless creatures.
We are helping the street dogs over the months.
We have started from Dehradun with handful of volunteers. Now we are giving our hand for a paw everywhere.
We feed the dogs on regular basis and there is proper check on the health of dogs.
Our team is working 24*7 for the four paws.
We rescue, vaccinate, sterilize, and facilitate adoption.
We are treating numerous dogs at this moment and trying to reach as much as we can. In addition to this we are in collaboration with many cafes and a tech-based company.

our services


As of now we rescue, vaccinate, sterilize, and facilitate adoption.
But we will soon expand and start with the shelter. We have trained medical team who look after the dogs and their health issues.
